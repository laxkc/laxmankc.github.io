Industra AI is an agent platform for industrial Q&A workloads. It accepts both text and image inputs, uses tool-calling for structured actions, and retrieves over a hybrid index combining BM25 lexical search with vector similarity. Every turn produces grounded answers with citations, a confidence signal, and a per-turn cost breakdown — designed for predictable behavior in production.
