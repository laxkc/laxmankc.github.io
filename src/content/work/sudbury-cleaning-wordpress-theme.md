A bespoke WordPress theme built for a Sudbury-based cleaning service, written from the ground up without relying on page builders. Service offerings are driven by a dynamic services system rather than hardcoded pages, the blog runs on standard WP post types, and the contact form is hardened against spam and injection. Templates and asset loading are optimized for performance on low-end client devices.
